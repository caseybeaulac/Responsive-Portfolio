About Me

My name is Casey, I was born and raised in Montreal, Canada where I spent most of my life. I graduated highschool in 2012 and immediatly went to college to study in social sciences. After a little over a year I decided to drop out. This program was not for me. I decided to jump into a coding class, something I had never done before.

After 3 years of writing lines and debugging I finally finished this program and did an internship for 2 months to aquire external experience. The internship went very well but unfortunatly the company did not had the budget to hire me. A few month later, I decided to pack my things and drive down to Orlando where I now live.


Here I am, taking this new coding class to improve my skills even further and potentialy launch my career as a full-stack developer. But for now I currently work as a server in a wine bar downtown Orlando trying to make the best out of it. Oh and this is a picture of my cat (who is also Canadian).
